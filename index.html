About

The Researcher

I'm a Master's student in Applied Machine Learning at the University of Maryland, College Park, graduating May 2026. My work sits at the intersection of robotics, preference learning, and multimodal AI systems.

Currently building scalable robotic preference datasets and advancing VLA-style preference learning pipelines — designing evaluation frameworks that integrate visual, language, and trajectory signals for complex multimodal decision-making.

I care about AI that works in the real world: reliable, interpretable, and efficient. From 80% model compression to state-of-the-art satellite change detection, I build things that ship.
